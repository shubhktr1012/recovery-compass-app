# Explore Programs Rotation Design

Date: 2026-03-28

## Goal

Replace the old two-card smoking-only comparison with a broader programme discovery section that reflects the multi-category platform without showing all six programmes at once.

## Approved Direction

- Use three categories with two programmes visible per category.
- Rotate categories automatically on a timer.
- Keep manual category switching available through visible chips/tabs.
- Use the newer marketing names from the client docs.
- Use website-only durations for now:
  - `14-Day Sleep Reset`
  - `21-Day Energy Reset`

## Category Structure

1. `Break Habits`
   - `6-Day Compass Reset`
   - `90-Day Smoke Free Journey`

2. `Restore Balance`
   - `14-Day Sleep Reset`
   - `21-Day Energy Reset`

3. `Build Vitality`
   - `Men’s Vitality Reset Program`
   - `Radiance Journey`

## UX Notes

- Each category has a short supporting sentence.
- Each programme card shows a name, a short description, a duration, and three focus points.
- Autoplay should pause/reset cleanly when the user manually changes category.
- Homepage cards should stay editorial rather than paywall-like, so price should not be the dominant visual element.
